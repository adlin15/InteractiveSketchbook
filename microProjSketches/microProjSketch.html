Input Idea - Bubble clicking simulator, using a box with circles that appear in different sizes and colors. The bubbles will appear over time, or will appear all at once in the box. Change over time - bubbles pop after a certain time, pop as much bubbles as you can! The goal is to get as much as you can within a time limit. Friction? Delay? Limit? - Appear after 10-20 seconds, bubbles disappears after 5 seconds. Maybe add powerups in the future if it is possible. Intended feeling - Happy to pop bubbles, sad to see scores/game ends.
